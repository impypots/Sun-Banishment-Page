Mission

The Sun Banishment movement is about real-world change, simple as that. We want legislative action, not empty platitudes. Our ultimate goal is to make our government discontinue the use of execution, in favor of replacing it with the more humane* approach of shooting death row inmates directly into the Sun.

How It Works

Firstly, it is important to note that the recipients of this new solution are patients, not inmates. The heart of this process is reconciliatory, not draconian. Every patient is provided free therapy sessions preceding their journey, in order to help them come to terms with the new direction their life is taking (directly into the Sun).

The vessel is equipped with an automated sound system that plays relaxing music to settle the patient. It also has a blanket (secondhand), enough food and water to last the entire trip, and a calming poster that reads “DON’T PANIC” in a welcoming bright red.

The day the patient reaches the local area of our Sun is called the Day of Forgiveness because on this day every patient is pardoned for any and all crimes committed. If they can somehow escape the gravitational pull of the Bringer of Life and make it back to earth, they will be welcomed back as free citizens.
